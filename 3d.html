About Us

At Dodeka, we utilize 3D modeling and virtual reality to create immersive and unique digital experiences. This includes everything from virtual historical tours to 3D product demos and training programs.

We're more than service providers. Our mission is to preserve history digitally. By using our technological skills, we reconstruct historical sites, people, and events, ensuring their longevity for future generations. We're dedicated to making history accessible to all through digital technology, no matter where or when.
